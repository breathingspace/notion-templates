About Notion

"Notion is a single space where you can think, write, and plan." It's so popular because you can easily use it in businesses, to manage projects, or just to take notes for yourself.

Are these templates free?

All templates we showcase will have a "Free" or "Paid" tag aswell as links to the creator's socials.

How do I use them?

Once you've opened the template on notion.so, there will be a "Duplicate" button in the top-right corner of the page.

Where do you find these templates?

Lots of templates are actually available straight from notion.so/templates, but this list is curated from templates I've found on Pinterest and Reddit, as well as the Notion site.

All templates are appropriately credited.
